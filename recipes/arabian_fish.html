SPICED ARABIAN FISH

Description

The spiced Arabian fish is one of the top cuisines in the world. You will find this food mostly in the middle Eastern countries due to the influence of the Arabian culture and the religion of Islam

Quite oftenly, the Spiced Arabian fish will also be found in restaurants and hotels of major cities around the world. This is because ore people around the world are embracing foods from different cultures due to globalization.

Ingredients

  • Tuna fish
  • Ground Paper
  • Onions
  • Marinator
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • cooking oil
  • Coriandor
  • Ripe Lemon

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the Fish
  2. Deepfry in hot oil
  3. Wait till the fish is brown
  4. Transfer on a plate
  5. Add Salt
  6. Add Corandor and Lemon
  7. Serve with raw onions
